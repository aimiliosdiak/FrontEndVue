About

Online editor allows you to edit documents collaboratively in real-time, much like a live multi-player editor that runs in your browser. Write articles, press releases, to-do lists, etc. together with your friends, fellow students or colleagues, all working on the same document at the same time.

Was designed in Figma and written in VueJS,HTML,CSS concerning the Front-end part.Other other hand Back-end piece of project written in GO language.
